## NACL Fixes Applied (post-cutover diagnosis)

Root cause: NACLs were written for direct VPC-to-VPC flows. After cutover, all
traffic transits VPC-B trust subnet (10.1.2.0/24) — the TGW attachment subnet.
NACLs did not allow port 80 transit through VPC-B, and did not allow traffic
arriving from 10.1.2.0/24 at VPC-C.

## Known Issue for Skill Files
NACLs governing transit subnets must allow traffic from the TGW attachment subnet
CIDR (10.1.2.0/24), not just the originating VPC CIDR. When VPC-B becomes a transit
inspection point, all inter-VPC traffic arrives at nacl-b-trust from the TGW ENI,
and arrives at destination VPC NACLs from 10.1.2.0/24 — not from the original source.
